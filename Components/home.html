Overview

Uni Admin is an education app that provides a platform for students to access course content, submit assignments, and interact with other students. The app is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, with a focus on simplicity and ease of use.

Key Features

Course Content

Students can access course announcements, lectures, and assignments through the app. This section is organized to keep students up-to-date with all course-related information.

Course Resources

Access resources such as textbooks, past papers, and other study materials directly through the app. All the resources are available in one centralized location.

Assignments

View and submit assignments with ease. Assignments are organized by course, allowing students to keep track of their academic responsibilities.

Notifications

Receive instant notifications for new announcements, assignment deadlines, and important updates to ensure that nothing is missed.

Profile Management

Manage your personal information and track your academic progress through a dedicated profile section.

Settings

Customize your app experience by adjusting notification preferences, choosing between light and dark modes, and more.

Uni Chat

Engage with peers, ask questions, and collaborate on projects in the Uni Chat section. Create study groups and share resources effortlessly.

Announcements

Stay informed about university events, holidays, and other important updates through the announcements feature.

Support

Contact our support team for assistance with technical issues, account problems, or other concerns. We're here to help!

Feedback

Provide feedback, suggest new features, and report bugs. Your input helps us improve the app for everyone.

Logout

Securely log out of the app to ensure your account's safety when not in use.

Dark Mode

Switch to dark mode for a more comfortable viewing experience in low-light conditions.

Our Goal

We aim to empower students to take control of their education by providing a user-friendly and intuitive app. Our goal is to help students succeed in their academic journey by making learning accessible, engaging, and efficient.

We are committed to continuous improvement and welcome feedback from our users to enhance the app's functionality and user experience.
